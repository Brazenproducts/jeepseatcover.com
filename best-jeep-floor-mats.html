Best Jeep Floor Mats

WeatherTech, Husky, Mopar, and 3D MAXpider ranked for Wrangler JL, JK, and Gladiator JT.

Updated April 2026 · 4 top picks · All-weather protection focus

A Jeep without good floor mats is a mistake you notice every time you hose out the interior. Mud season, sand season, beach season, dog season — Jeeps accumulate stuff that destroys factory carpet in a hurry. The right floor mats turn the worst interior messes into a 10-minute cleanup instead of a detailing nightmare.

These are the best options for Wrangler JL, JK, and Gladiator JT platforms.

Our Top Picks for Jeep Floor Mats

#1 Pick

WeatherTech Floor Liners

★★★★★ 5.0/5
$$$ · All-weather · Best overall protection

WeatherTech is the benchmark for Jeep floor protection. Their precision-fit floor liners cover the footwells completely, trap mud and water in deep channels, and are easy to remove and hose out. They are the reason WeatherTech has dominated this category for decades.

    Pros

  • Precision vehicle-specific fitment
  • Deep channeling for water and debris
  • Easy to remove and clean
  • Hard-wearing material
  • Backed by a strong warranty

    Cons

  • Premium price
  • Can feel less premium than some alternatives
  • Available mainly in black

Runner Up

Husky Liners Floor Mats

★★★★☆ 4.7/5
$$ · All-weather · Great value

Husky Liners are the strong second choice that often costs less than WeatherTech while delivering nearly identical protection. They have been the go-to alternative for Jeep owners who want serious floor coverage without the WeatherTech premium.

    Pros

  • Excellent all-weather coverage
  • More affordable than WeatherTech
  • Good channeling design
  • Rugged, durable material
  • Lifetime warranty

    Cons

  • Fit can be slightly less precise than WeatherTech
  • Less refined finish around edges

Best OE Fit

Mopar Floor Mats

★★★★☆ 4.5/5
$$ · OEM choice · Factory-engineered fit

Mopar floor mats come straight from Jeep and fit every contour perfectly because they were designed by the same engineers who designed the vehicle. For owners who prioritize a precise factory look and a known brand, this is the smart choice.

    Pros

  • Perfect factory fitment
  • Designed by Jeep for Jeep
  • Easy to order for your exact model
  • OE quality materials

    Cons

  • Can be more expensive than aftermarket
  • Fewer color and style options
  • Less aggressive channeling than dedicated aftermarket

Premium Carpet Pick

3D MAXpider Floor Mats

★★★★☆ 4.4/5
$$ · 3D laser-scanned fit · Premium look

3D MAXpider uses 3D laser scanning to achieve an extremely precise fit. They look more refined than rubber mats and feel better underfoot, which some Jeep owners prefer for daily-driving over the pure utility of rubberized options.

    Pros

  • 3D laser-scanned precision fit
  • More refined appearance
  • Better-feeling surface
  • Kicker layer prevents slipping

    Cons

  • Less aggressive than rubber all-weather mats
  • More maintenance than rubber options
  • Not ideal for heavy mud season use

The Bottom Line

For serious all-weather Jeep use, WeatherTech is still the category leader. Husky Liners is the strong value alternative with lifetime warranty backing. Mopar is the right choice when you want exact OE fitment, and 3D MAXpider is best for owners who want something more refined for daily-driver use.
