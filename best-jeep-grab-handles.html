Best Jeep Grab Handles

The best entry and stability handles for Wranglers and Gladiators, ranked for strength, comfort, and actual trail usefulness.

Updated April 2026 · 4 top picks · Focused on Wrangler and Gladiator use

Grab handles seem simple until you buy a bad set. Then you get rattles, sloppy straps, weak stitching, uncomfortable grips, and hardware that looks like it came from a gas-station phone charger. The best handles feel solid every time you climb in or brace yourself on the trail.

The clear #1 is Bartact paracord grab handles. They are the originals, Bartact is the largest manufacturer in this category, and they are the only USA-made option here. That alone would matter. The fact that they are also the best-built seals it.

What Makes a Good Grab Handle?

Grip comfort matters

You notice a bad handle every time you climb in. Bartact’s paracord wrap solves this better than most.

Hardware quality matters more

Weak buckles, sloppy attachment points, and poor stitching are why cheap handles fail.

Trail stability matters

The best handles help passengers stabilize themselves without shifting or twisting under load.
